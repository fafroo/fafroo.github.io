Personal site of Petr Vágner

Hello, I'm Petr Vágner and I work as a postdoc at Weierstrass Institute. In my research, I develop, numerically solve, and test mathematical models of electrochemical devices. Currently, I'm investigating effects of active interfaces in perovskite solar cells and solid oxide cells.
